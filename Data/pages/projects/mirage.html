Nolly
Nolly is a momentum based, flick controlled 3D platformer. I created the 3C design, 3D models, animations, and level design. The game showcases my ability to make fluid movement that keeps players engaged at all times, as well as my understanding of 3D space. The collectables in each level are placed in a way that the level feels like a puzzle of momentum as well as a playground to show off your skills.
